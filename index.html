Privacy Policy for FootBuzz

FootBuzz is a personal application created to automatically post football-related content to the FootBuzz Facebook Page.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

It only accesses the Facebook Page (FootBuzz) owned by the developer to manage and publish football content.

No data is shared with third parties.
